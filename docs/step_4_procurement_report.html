1. Executive Summary

This report details the procurement process for five mobile phone models: Samsung Galaxy A54 5G, iPhone 13 Pro Max, Google Pixel 7, Xiaomi Redmi Note 12 Pro, and OnePlus Nord N30 5G. 2. Introduction

The purpose of this report is to investigate and compare the prices of five specified mobile phone models from various online retailers in Egypt. The scope includes identifying potential vendors and analyzing available pricing data.

Price data was gathered by querying popular Egyptian e-commerce websites (Amazon.eg, Jumia.com.eg, Noon.com, Mobile57.com) using specific search terms.
